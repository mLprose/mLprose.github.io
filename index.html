Active Theory

Active Theory is a studio that designs websites, AR and VR experiences, apps, and real world electronic installation experiences. Many of the elements on the studio homepage shift perspective slightly as you move your cursor around the landing page. On other project pages a large distortion effect follows the cursor similar to what we looked at early in the term.

Kaspersky Cybermap

Kaspersky's Cyberattack map is a live visualizer of active cyber threats and attacks. You can spin the globe around, specify what cyber threats to display, and see the source of their data. The green and black UI along with the neon colored visualizations make it an entertaining site to try.

One Hour One Life

One hour one life is a formerly free online multiplayer game where you play through single hour long human lives at a time. You can communicate with others you run into, and you try to develop the best life you can for yourself starting from nothing before you automatically bear another player as a baby and die soon after. It is a unique experience that has an art style with lots of character.
